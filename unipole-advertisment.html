Introduction

What is Unipole Advertising?

Unipole advertising is a single pole large billboard format built for high-visibility outdoor presence. The elevated structure keeps the brand readable from long distances, making it useful for city entry points, major junctions, highways and busy commercial routes. For campaigns in Uttarakhand, unipole sites help brands create premium recall, stronger road visibility and steady exposure across daily commuter, tourist and local traffic movement.
