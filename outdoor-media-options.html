FAQ

Questions brands usually ask before booking outdoor inventory.

If you need campaign planning support for launches, route dominance or premium city entries, we can suggest the best mix based on region, budget and duration.

Which outdoor format is best for maximum impact? +

For strong scale and prestige, unipoles and large-format billboards perform best. Gantries work especially well at city entries, while highway hoardings are ideal for long-route campaigns.

Can you help select locations across multiple cities? +

Yes. We can recommend a route-first mix across Uttarakhand, Punjab, Western UP and Rajasthan so the campaign feels connected instead of fragmented.

Do you support campaign planning for pilgrimage and tourism routes? +

Yes. We regularly consider movement patterns around pilgrimage circuits, tourism corridors and major highways where travel traffic creates repeated exposure.

How do you make outdoor look more premium? +

Premium impact comes from the right structure, correct viewing angle, uncluttered creative and smart clustering of sites instead of isolated random placements.
