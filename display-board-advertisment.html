Introduction

What are sun boards (display boards)?

Sun boards are rigid printed panels (often used like foam boards) that carry retail messaging on walls, racks and counters. They are popular because they look neat, print sharply and work for both short campaigns and everyday store communication.

Rigid & Neat

Stable panels with clean edges for a premium in-store look.

High Clarity

Sharp prints that make offers and features readable quickly.

Easy Mount

Suitable for wall pasting, framing, hanging or stand mounts.

Outlet Ready

Quick replication across dealer and retail networks.

FAQ

FAQs about sun boards advertising

Quick answers about board types, placements and what makes an in-shop board look premium.

Where should a display board be placed inside a store? +

Near entry points, product zones, billing counters and high-dwell areas where customers naturally look.

Are sun boards good for short campaigns? +

Yes. They are great for festive offers, launches and limited-time schemes because production and rollout is fast.

How do you keep boards consistent across multiple outlets? +

We use a standard layout system, fixed sizes and repeatable design rules so every outlet looks uniform.

What makes a board look premium? +

Clean typography, short messaging, good contrast, correct mounting height and sharp finishing on edges.
