Introduction

What are flex boards and dealer boards?

Flex boards are printed signage panels used for shopfronts, dealer points and outlet branding. Dealer boards are designed for consistency—so every location looks like part of the same brand network with matching colors, logo placement and layout hierarchy.

FAQ

FAQs about flex boards and dealer boards

Quick answers about consistency, rollout timelines, sizing and what makes dealer branding work better.

How do you keep all dealer boards consistent? +

We standardize a single layout template (logo, offer, dealer info) and maintain print rules so every outlet looks uniform.

What is the best size for dealer boards? +

Size depends on frontage width and viewing distance. We recommend sizes that keep the logo and main message readable.

Can you do multi-location rollouts quickly? +

Yes. We batch print, route plan installations and schedule outlet-wise fitting for faster rollouts.

Can dealer boards be combined with glow signs? +

Yes. Glow signs improve night visibility, and dealer boards maintain consistent daytime branding—together they build stronger storefront identity.
