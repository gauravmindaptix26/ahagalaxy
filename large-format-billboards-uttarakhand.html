FAQ

FAQs about large format billboards in Uttarakhand

Quick answers about locations, creative best practices, pricing factors and execution timelines.

When should a brand choose a large format billboard? +

Choose it when the campaign needs high-impact visibility, launch-scale attention and a premium outdoor statement in one glance.

What type of creative performs best on large billboards? +

Bold simple creative with one main message, one strong visual anchor and high contrast performs best from a distance.

Do large billboards work for local campaigns too? +

Yes. Local brands benefit when they want to appear bigger, more established and harder to miss in their target city.

Can large billboards be combined with other OOH formats? +

Yes. Large billboards work best with highway hoardings, gantry branding and unipoles to build full-route campaign coverage.
