What are Large Format Billboards?

Large format billboards are premium outdoor structures designed for mass visibility, high recall and strong first impression. They work best on city corridors, approach roads and route-heavy zones where the campaign needs to look big, urgent and instantly readable.

What is the cost of large format billboards in Uttarakhand? +

Pricing depends on billboard location, size and duration. Share your target city, route and campaign timeline to get a custom quote.

Which locations are best for large format billboards? +

The best location depends on approach angle, traffic weight and audience type. City entry points, junctions and highway approaches usually deliver strong recall.

How long does installation take? +

After finalizing the location and artwork, printing and installation typically happens quickly depending on site readiness and production requirements.
