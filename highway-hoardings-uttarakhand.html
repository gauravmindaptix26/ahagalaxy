Introduction

What are Highway Hoardings?

Highway hoardings are outdoor billboard structures placed on intercity routes, approach roads and tourism corridors. The format performs best when the audience is in motion for long durations—drivers, passengers, tourists and pilgrims—creating repeated exposure and stronger brand recall through route repetition.

FAQ

FAQs about highway hoardings in Uttarakhand

Quick answers about pricing, routes, creative guidelines and campaign planning.

Which routes are best for highway hoardings? +

The best route depends on your audience—tourism, pilgrimage or intercity commuters. We recommend corridors where travel time, approach angle and traffic volume match your campaign goal.

How should creative be designed for highway visibility? +

Use big visuals, short copy and high contrast. Highway viewers process information quickly, so a clean hierarchy performs best.

Is a single board enough for a highway campaign? +

One board can work, but multiple boards spaced along a route create much stronger recall through repeated exposure.

Can highway hoardings be combined with city formats? +

Yes. Highway visibility becomes stronger when paired with gantry branding at entry points and unipoles inside the city to complete the memory loop.
