FAQ

Questions before choosing highway hoardings in Uttarakhand.

Highway formats are most effective when the audience is route-based and the campaign objective is broad awareness with repeated road exposure.

What type of brand benefits most from highway hoardings? +

Brands targeting large regional audiences, travelers, tourists, pilgrims and intercity commuters benefit the most from highway hoardings.

Are highway hoardings suitable for product launches? +

Yes. They are useful when the launch needs broad regional awareness rather than only city-level exposure.

How should creative be designed for highway visibility? +

Use big visuals, short copy and a clean hierarchy so the message can be understood quickly at speed.

Can highway boards be combined with city formats? +

Yes. Highway hoardings often work best when combined with city entries or local formats to close the campaign memory loop.
